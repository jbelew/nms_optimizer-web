What is an Adjacency Bonus?

In No Man's Sky, an adjacency bonus is a crucial mechanic that boosts the stats of your technology modules. When you place compatible technology modules next to each other in your inventory, they form a synergy and receive a performance boost. The NMS Optimizer is the perfect tool to figure out the best arrangement for maximum adjacency bonus.

What Are Supercharged Slots?
